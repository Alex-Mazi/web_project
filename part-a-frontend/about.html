About Us

IT Library is a student-driven platform dedicated to providing accessible and high-quality learning resources to anyone interested in computer science and technology. Founded in 2025 by Alexandra Mazi and Christina Perifana, the platform aims to empower learners of all levels by curating a wide range of courses, books, and practical resources.

Our team is passionate about making technology understandable and fun for everyone. The rapid evolution of technology presents challenges to self-taught learners, which is why we strive to create a space where students can find clear learning pathways and reliable recommendations. We believe that with the right resources and support, anyone can develop the skills needed to thrive in the tech world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make tech education more accessible by providing free and affordable access to quality learning materials. We are committed to fostering a supportive community where learners can grow their skills, gain confidence, and achieve their goals in the ever-changing field of technology.

  • Accessible Education for All: We are dedicated to ensuring everyone has access to quality learning resources.
  • Focus on Real-World Relevance: We ensure all content is practical and directly applicable to industry needs.
  • Supportive Learning Community: We foster a collaborative environment where learners can support each other and grow together.
